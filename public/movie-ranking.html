The Best Movies According to Abdo!

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

1. The Dead Poets Society

Great inspirational movie that reminds us of what life is really all about.

2. Coach Carter

A sports movie that will definitely get you motivated to achieve your potential.

3. The Boy and the Beast

Touching anime movie that explains the story of a little boy who is found and taken care of by a beast who also teaches him martial arts.
